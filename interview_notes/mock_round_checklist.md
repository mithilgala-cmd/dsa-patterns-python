# Mock Round Checklist

## Before round
- Pick 2 problems (1 medium + 1 easy fallback).
- Set a strict timer (45 minutes total).
- Disable hints/editorials.

## During round
- Spend max 5 minutes on problem understanding and examples.
- Write brute force idea quickly.
- Move to optimized approach with complexity target.
- Keep 5 minutes at end for test cases and edge cases.

## After round
- Log each problem in `problem_tracker.csv`.
- Tag mistake category (logic, edge case, complexity, syntax, panic).
- Schedule next revision date.
- Write one line: "What I will do differently next time".
